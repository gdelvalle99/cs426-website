About our project


The main goal for the project is to develop a robotic docent in order to try to simulate the functions of a human docent in order to navigate to exhibits and pieces to deliver information for clients of the museum and to make the experience as interactive as possible. In terms of progress, the development team has started with making a prototype. The team was able to spend a work day in order to work on setting up the development environment. In addition, our team was able to work on scheduling standups on a weekly basis to get quicker results on developing the prototype. The development team is split into the robotics team and the web interface team to properly utilize the team member’s skillsets. The development team will have a robotics team to work closely with the robot’s navigation, obstacle avoidance, mapping, and orientation while the interface team will work on interfacing a map editor to collect museum data such as exhibits and pieces to send at the robot’s request. In addition, the editor will contain more information such as analytics and data pertaining to what the robot can query based on statistics (distance traveled, time used, amounts initiated, etc).
